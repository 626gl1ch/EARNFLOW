Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get paid?

Withdrawals are processed automatically via Paystack directly to your Nigerian bank account or mobile money account. Minimum withdrawal is 1,000 NGN.

No! EarnFlow is 100% free forever. We pay you for completing offers, surveys, and tasks sponsored by advertisers.

Your earnings depend on the time you dedicate and the tasks available in your country. Regular users earn anywhere from ₦2,000 to ₦15,000 daily plus referral commissions.
